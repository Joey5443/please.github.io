Donate

Annual Fund

Gifts from alumni, parents,friends, and anyone interested in art make an immediate impact on the School.Scholarship Funds are the top priority and the money recieved from the Annual Fund donations directly suppors our students. Gift of all sizes make an impact.

Endowment Funds

Gift commitments of $50,000 or more (over a period of up to five years) can be directed to the Yale Endowment to provide on going support for the school of Art.These gifts are invested into growing steadily overtime and providing more and more distributed income to the school each year.

Endowment gifts can support scholarships,faculty, research,travel and capital needs.To discuss making an endowment:

  • Please contact: Jill Westgard our Director of Development at Yale School of Art
  • E-mail: jill.weestgard@yale.edu
  • phone: 1(203) 432-5846

Planned Giving

You can support the school through bequest, annuities, trusts and other financial vehicles.

Surprises are nice and definetly welcomed, but if possible please be sure to contact the Director of Development at the Yale School of Art to discuss your planned gift. This inturn helps insure that your vision is carried out as intended.

It is important to be clear and state specifically what programs you would like to support in regards to school i.e. scholarships, students, faculty and so on. It is strongly recommended to assertain your declaration:"I give dollars and or intrust assets to Yale Art Students at Yale University located in New Haven, Connecticut." This ensures legally that there are no errors and or misdistribution of current and future donations.
