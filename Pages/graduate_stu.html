Graduate Studies

Graphic Design(MFA)

The graphic design program focuses on the development of a cohesive, investigative body of work also known as the student's thesis.At Yale the graphic design thesis is concieved as a loose framework within each student's.

Each Course in the program is designed to replicate real world work environments. Students are assigned job tasks simulating working with teams as well as free lance one on one with personal clients.

Painting And PrintMaking(MFA)

Instruction in these programs are rooted in a unique genre with its own complex syntax and history. Within the setting this program encourages diversity of representation and interpretations of art.

In Painting students will learn various techniques and stylized approach. Artists will dive into free form expression, abstract art, portrait, landscape, mural painting hands on approach. Students will use various mix media tools. Classes are structured to give good understanding of art style and guide students to discover what approach they would like to master.

Printmaking will dive into trending topics and use of media in mutiple products. Students will learn how to apply their printmaking skills to create and sell trending products.

Sculpture(MFA)

The sculpture program offers students the opportunity to develop their work in a supportive environment consisting of critical feedback in a broad array of diverse voices. Students will learn thru sculpture various use of molds, clay and materials currently used in the mainstream industry and learn to adapt to the demans of a rapidly changing environment.

Photography(MFA)

Photography is a two year program of study admiting ten student's a year.Darkroom,studio, and computer facilities are provided.Students receive technical instruction in black and white as well as color. Photography techniques will be shown in traditional and digital format for range of use to students for free lance and professional work.
