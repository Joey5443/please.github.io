Summer Studies

The Artist Collective

Started in 2019, the Artist Collective Summer School is a two-week program for current MFA students.

It's in partnership with the Paul Mellon Centre, the Yale Art History Department and Center for British Art.

Yale Norfolk School of Art

Established in 1984, the Yale Norfolk School of Art is an intensive six-week undergraduate summer residency program currently home to 26 rising seniors.

Yale Summer Session

Undergraduate-level courses are offered at the Yale School of Art through Yale summer session.

Always looking for more applicants to join in this fast paced, fun and challenging environment which will help students reach new heights.
